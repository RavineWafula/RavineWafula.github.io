I am a fifth year Electrical and Electronic Engineering student at the University of Nairobi with a passion for technology, embedded systems and programming. Currently, I am undertaking my attachment at GEARBOX with a hope to learn more in my area of interest.

Project 1: Smart Irrigation System

This is project provides an intelligent and water efficient solution to large scale irrigation in arid and semi-arid areas. It relys on soil moisture and humidity sensors, temperature sensors and information from a weather forecasting cloud service to decide when to irrigate. Also, it supports remote actuation of tap valve to irrigate the farm as and when needed.

Project 2: Vehicle Remote Monitoring System

The aim of this project is to offer a way to monitor the "health" of ones vehicle(s) remotely. It makes use of an on board diagnostics (OBD) which is usually installed in most vehicles and the data gotten is interpreted and transmitted to a central cloud storage. One can therefore access this information through a web or mobile app remotely.

Project 3: Smart Wash

This project targets car wash owners and provides three main solutions to challenges such people face in managing their businesses remotely. The challenges are: Lost revenue due to defraudment by car wash employees, lack of bussiness intelligence and analytics, and lack of proper accountability.
